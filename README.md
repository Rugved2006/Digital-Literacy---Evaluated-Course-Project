Digital Literacy: Evaluated Course Project

📌 Project Overview

This repository contains the evaluated project for the Digital Literacy course. The goal of this project is to explore and demonstrate a comprehensive understanding of how to safely, effectively, and critically navigate the modern digital landscape.

It covers the essential pillars of digital intelligence, including misinformation awareness, online safety, and professional digital communication.

🚀 Key Topics Covered

*   Critical Thinking: Identifying "fake news," lateral reading, and source verification.
    
*   Online Safety: Managing digital footprints, privacy settings, and phishing prevention.
    
*   Productivity Tools: Utilization of collaborative platforms for student success.
    
*   Netiquette: Professional email etiquette and healthy social media engagement.

🛠️ Essential Digital Tools

The project explores the following tools to enhance academic and professional workflows:

*   Productivity: Google Workspace, Notion, Evernote.
    
*   Creativity: Canva, Adobe Express.
    
*   Safety: Bitwarden (Password Management), DuckDuckGo (Private Search).
    

🎓 Conclusion

Digital literacy is not just a technical skill—it is a mindset. This project demonstrates the ability to differentiate between credible and non-credible information while maintaining a secure and professional online presence.
